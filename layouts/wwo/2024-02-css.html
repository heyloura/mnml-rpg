This is an ode to before.

A time when the HTML standard allowed you to style a webpage.
You were not burdened with adding a style sheet, and table layouts reigned supreme.
Now such whimsy disappears.

Others are warned away from this path:
ignore the deprecations at your own peril.

The height of excellence

The humble marquee tag

100% proven to distract
your website visitors
